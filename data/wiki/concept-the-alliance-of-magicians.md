The Alliance of Magicians is an organization of professional magicians who take their craft extremely seriously. It was founded by G.O.B., but he was expelled after his Aztec Tomb illusion was revealed on the news. Their slogan is: "We Demand to be Taken Seriously"

## Appearances
"]]
- "Pilot" - The Alliance is introduced, and G.O.B. is expelled for revealing the Aztec Tomb illusion. G.O.B. is passed up for a Teen Magician who is still alliance approved.
- "Storming the Castle" - G.O.B. attempts to convince Rollo that he should be allowed back in the alliance by performing an act at the Gothic Castle, but is kicked out because his assistants, George Michael and Marta, are not Alliance approved.
- "Let 'Em Eat Cake" - Michael mentions that perhaps The Alliance of Magicians could pick up some slack and support the Bluth family instead of him.
- "Sword of Destiny" - G.O.B. enlists Buster to perform "The Sword of Destiny" illusion in order to get back into the Alliance.
