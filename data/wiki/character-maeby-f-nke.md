Mae "Maeby" Fünke is portrayed by Alia Shawkat and appeared in 76 episodes of Arrested Development. She is last seen in "The Fallout".

## About
Maeby (born September 22, 1990) is the biological daughter of Lindsay and Tobias Fünke. Despite her parents' lack of skill and talent, Maeby is an accomplished con artist. She has an alter ego named Surely Fünke, a wheelchair bound girl suffering from a disease called BS, which she uses to get money through donations. She also cons her way into being a movie executive at Tantamount Studios for a couple of years.

Maeby is the on-and-off object of her cousin George Michael's affection and the two briefly experiment with incestuous behaviors. Maeby is also strongly attracted to Steve Holt, who, unfortunately, is also her cousin.

### Careers

Upon hearing about George Michael's Fakeblock project, Maeby decided to wedge her way into the ground floor and began raising investment funds, until she was abruptly fired.

### Season four
]]

Immediately after the Queen Mary sank, Maeby tried to get her parents' attention by failing her senior year of high school while simultaneously getting a job at Imagine Entertainment. When her parents went out of town, she took a film crew to India to shoot Gangie on the Ganges. While in India, she spotted her mother at the Four Seasons Mumbai, so she posed as a shaman to convince her mother to return and pay attention to her.

Upon returning to the States, Maeby learned she had been fired from Image for not having her diploma. She decided to go back to high school, but kept flunking out hoping to get her parents' attention. After they abandoned her for Marky Bark and DeBrie she decided to make a career of remaining a high school senior at various schools. In her fifth senior year she adopted the last name Featherbottom, the surname of her father's British nanny alter ego. She decided to finally graduate high school but realized she needed math tutoring. Her tutor ends up being her cousin George Michael.

At that time Maeby learned there was an undercover cop at her school. As Maeby was 23 many suspected it was her but after spotting a student named Perfecto Telles flashing a badge in a bar Maeby suspected it was him. After being told by Barry Zuckerkorn that she risked being expelled for being over 21 she decides to seduce Pefecto so she wouldn't be busted.

Meanwhile she helped George Michael turn Fakeblock into a legitimate company until she overhyped it too much and he fired her. She also acted as a pimp for the unwitting Lindsay who was sleeping with Herbert Love.

Maeby told her suspicions to Perfecto about him being an undercover cop. He confirmed that he was and they ended up having sex, and were accidentally seen by his foster mum Lucille Austero. At Cinco de Cuatro Maeby found out Perfecto was lying and was only 17 years old. The badge he had flashed earlier was one that proclaimed him to be a member of the unimpressive "anti-bullying brigade". Days later at school Maeby was arrested for sleeping with a minor and was last seen cringing at the prospect of having to be forced to move to Sudden Valley due to California's strict sex offender laws.

Maeby's focal episode in Season Four is Señoritis. She also appears in the episodes Flight of the Phoenix, Indian Takers, The B. Team, A New Start, "Red Hairing", "It Gets Better", "Off the Hook", "Blockheads".

### Season five
Despite getting arrested, Maeby somehow avoids jail time and heads to Lucille Austero's penthouse to clean it out of her stuff, eluding Michael Bluth in the process.  She meets up with George Michael in the process in the courtyard to discuss his recourse over he and his father's relationship with Rebel Alley.

They eventually drive out to Sudden Valley looking for Michael but instead run into Steve Holt, the three of them heading to Mexico where Maeby finds a life helping the locals and learns that FakeBlock isn't real.  She eventually returns home to help support her mother's run for political office and hopefully ruin it, but her attempts only make Lindsay more popular.  

Now looking for a place to live, Maeby disguises herself as an older woman to live at Lucille 2's house at the Spotted Palm Retirement Community, but she instead gets stuck with Stan Sitwell, Lucille's old roommate  and tries to get rid of him. Tobias, DeBrie and Murphy-Brown soon move in as well. When they vote her out, she discovers that Stan was eliminating her fellow tenants, and moves to a different empty house in the community.  

Meanwhile, Maeby and George Michael are trying to unload themselves of FakeBlock and after someone buys it, they learn that Michael has purchased it,  later passing it on to the Chinese to cover his parents' debt to them. 

## Trivia
- Maeby does not appear in the episode "For British Eyes Only" except for archival footage.
- In Season Four, Maeby only appears in archive footage in "Borderline Personalities" and "Colony Collapse". She does not appear in the episodes "Double Crossers", "Smashed", "Queen B.", and "A New Attitude".
- Maeby doesn't appear in the Season Five episode "Courting Disasters"
- Maeby is technically George Michael's first cousin once removed since Lindsay turned out to be Lucille's half-sister (meaning that Lucille is her half-aunt and Michael is her first cousin).
